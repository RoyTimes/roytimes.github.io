{PIN}  C++ Effective STL(3)

终于要填这个坑了。之所以和上一篇距离那么远是因为之后的一个东西叫做Allocator ，这个东西大概是C++历史上最耻辱的发明之一。非常难过的一个东西，设计的非常逗比，最开始是为了抽象出来内存分配的类，但是由于最后实在是无法自圆其说，于是C++的STL组委会妥协了，允许STL的实现者可以不采用Allocator来作为其容器的默认构造器。简单说就是为了类似于发明了一种杯子装果汁，之后杯子设计的不好，直接让人不用杯子直接喝的感觉。非常煞笔。这一期可能我会一直讲这个难过的东西，但是也并没有什么办法。因为这东西就到了该说起来的地方了。

About HackCraft

What is hack

Hack is a special skill for awesome programmers to create a non reproducible but extremely excellent idea. Hacking is DEFINITELY NOT a bunch of computer guys sitting in front of the computer terminal trying to get illegal access into the CIA or FBI. It’s all about create awesome new things. It’s cool and it’s fun. Linus HACKed and created GNU/Linux system; Bjarne HACKed and created C++; Ryan HACKed and created Node.js. These people would be remember forever as great HACKERS. Why won’t you be the next?

What is Hackthon

Hackthon comes from the U.S. collage as a cool game played by the top coders in the computer science schools. It typically last for a few days and involve the coders to keep coding for 24 hours. Nowadays, Hackthon evolved into a complete collection of developing team involving UI (User interface) builders, UX(User Experience) builders, Coders, PM(Project Manager) and more, to create something new, like a new APP, in a few days by working together for 24 hours or 36 hours. Legitimately, nothing is cooler than being the best team in a Hackthon.

About HackCraft

A bunch of creative high school students from China created HackCraft, a unique type of hackthon, with completely revolutionary changes from the conventional hackthon. This hackthon game would located in a really awesome house in Shanghai with best facilities ever in the history of hackthon. Only 4 teams with no more than 30 competitors would be involved. For the first 24 hours, there would be a no stopping puzzle solving match, trying to solve as 50 challanging puzzles as much as possible. For the next 24 hours there would be the first round of new idea creating, during which your team would discuss about every details and began building your idea demo. Finally, comes the last 36 hours. Your team have to finish building your software. BEST ADVICE: To PM, MAKE DECISION FASTER. TO CODERS, CODE FASTER. TO UI/UX BUILDERS, CREATE INTERFACE FASTER. Finally, teamwork would be judge by 10 unprofessional college students and more than 3 professional professors (maybe teacher in the university or CTO of some famous cooperations). Final score would be counted according to score from these judge team and score from the puzzle solving contest.Moreover, for the most comfortable hacking experience for all the teams, there would be a suppliance committee serveing all teams with food, drinks and anything else. HAVE FUN HACKING. BE COOL HACKERS.

For infinite possibilities, visit http://hackcraft.ziorix.com for more info.
