About Renaissance Hack

Renaissance Hack is an competitive enrollment event for talented programmers and artists to create new age artwork with 21st-century technology together in a limited time. It's sponsored by some of the best-known companies in the industry. It's divided into two sections: Creating & Display. A group of programmers and artists is going to craft their project during the first section of the event. During the second section, the general public is allowed to visit and teams from the first section are supposed to make public-facing talk or art display (according to the specific type of their project).
